About Us

The Neuroscience Center of Acadiana is part of the largest non-profit, community-owned regional health system and is always commited to delivering excellent service to our patients. We were named a Center of Excellense in 2012. Our neurologists and sleep doctors diagnose and treat a wide spectrum of neurological conditions involivng the brain, spine, nerves, and muscle by using cutting edge technology and research. This allows our team of neurologists, nurses, and other clinicians to deliver innovative treatment approaches centered around our patients. 
We are proud use the latest technology in delivery the most innovative care to our patients with our Cyberknife robotic radiosurgery to treat tumors in the brain or elsewhere in the body without an incision, pain, or anesthesia. Cyberknife is able to destroy tumor cells by using highly targeted radiation.
