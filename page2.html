OUR AWESOME TEAM

Board certified in Sleep Medicine, Dr. Foreman is co-medical director of Sleep Center of Acadiana at Lafayette General. Other neurological areas of interest for Dr. Foreman include sleep disorders, headaches, neuromodulatory therapies (DBS, VNS, etc.), acupuncture, manual therapies and non-opioid pain management.

Adam Foreman, M.D.
Co-Medical Director of Sleep Center

Dr. Hargrave is the medical director of Neuroscience Center of Acadiana. He has 15 years of experience in private practice in, neurology and sleep medicine, Dr. Hargrave is also co-medical director of Sleep Center of Acadiana at Lafayette General. Other particular areas of interest for Dr. Hargrave include Parkinson's, MS, epilepsy, neuroimaging and the relationship between sleep apnea and stroke risk.
Accepting Medicare and most major insurances. 

Kevin Hargrave, M.D.
Medical Director of Neuroscience Center and Co-Medical Director of Sleep Center

Dr. Kellner makes her return to Acadiana to join Lafayette General Health's Neurology team at Neuroscience Center of Acadiana. She treats a variety of neurological conditions from migraines and epilepsy to neuropathy and more.

Virginia Kellner, M.D.

Dr. Patterson is certified by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology. He has expanded his practice from Lafayette to Crowley to better serve patients in Acadiana. He specializes in neurology, vascular neurology and neuro-interventional surgery, including treatment for chronic spine pain, strokes, brain aneurysms and other neurologic conditions.

Damon Patterson, M.D.
Medical Director of Stroke Center
